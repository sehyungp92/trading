Why the archived old mutations beat the direct post-fix run:
  The direct post-fix optimizer was path-dependent and the V5R1 score is R-based; it does not directly reward dollar PnL.
  It accepted sizing dampeners that helped score/DD tradeoffs but suppressed net dollars.
  The archived path preserved high-value route hygiene: CDD cap, route quarantine, and afternoon/VWAP tail-route shutdown.
  Under the fixed fill model those old route controls were still valid rather than stale.
